Hello World!

My name is Jack Yi but you guys can call me Jack. As a first jobber, I'm taking a leap of faith to change my career path from being a business analyst, to being a web designer/developer, as I just really love creating and designing pretty stuffs!

I only got exposed to designing when I was learning how to edit videos and thumbnails for my YouTube channel. Subsequently, I took up the role of Publicity Head in my university dance club, which requires me to design materials such as posters to merchandises such as tee-shirts and caps. I always treat designing as a hobby and nothing else till I discovered front-end development and that's when I thought: "Could this be something that I can persue for my career?". Hence I took up an introductory module on front-end development during my last year of uni and I absolutely loved the module. Fast forward to graduation, I followed the natural course of my business analytics major and took up the offer for a business analytics program. However throughout my first job, I could not stop thinking about web development and how it would be something that I would want to do for the rest of my life. Hence I decided just go for it, and hence I'm here kicking start my web developer learning journey by creating my first ever static website!
