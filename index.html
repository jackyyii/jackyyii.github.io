About Me

Just a little back story on why I chose to change my career path.

I am a business analytics graduate from National University of Singapore (NUS). Business analytics is a degree offered by School of Computing which thus provided me with exposure to coding languages such as Java, R, and mainly Python. During university, I was also the Publicity Head for my dance club which my main responsibility was to design publicity materials and merchandises for our club.

I was first exposed to designing when I was learning how to edit videos and thumbnails for my YouTube channel back in my teenage days. During those years, I learnt how to use Adobe Photoshop and Premiere Pro from the internet. Since then design has always been just a hobby. Even when applied for the role as a Publicity Head, designing was still something that I've never thought would be a possible career path to take.

Therefore, I was mainly focusing on my business analyst degree till I learnt about front-end development and web design from my colleagues during internship in my third year of uni. That's when I thought:

"Could this be something that I can pursue as a career?".
Hence I took up an introductory module on front-end development during my last year of uni and I must say I absolutely enjoyed the module and did well for it. You can view my assignments and my final project, where we had to create a game, here!

Fast forward to graduation, I followed the natural course of taking up an offer for a business analytics graduate program. However throughout my program, I could not stop thinking about doing web development as my career instead and how it would possibly be something that I would want to do for the rest of my life. So here I am, taking the leap of faith to work towards web development / design.
